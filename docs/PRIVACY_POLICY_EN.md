# Privacy Policy - Royal Door

## Privacy Commitment

Royal Door app respects your privacy and operates under Google Play requirements regarding transparency, data protection, and compliance with international laws, including GDPR.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Account Information**: Name, email, profile picture, and phone number upon verification.
- **User Content**: Messages, photos, videos, audio, and posts within the app.
- **Device Data**: Device type, operating system, language, app version.
- **Usage Data**: Your interaction with rooms, messages, games, features, and app performance.
- **Tracking and Analytics Data**: Ad ID, interaction with ads, and app performance measurement.
- **Transaction Data**: Recharge request information (transaction number, amount, date) - **saved for security and verification purposes only.**

---

## 2. How We Use Data

- To operate the app and core services like voice rooms, calls, and chat.
- For account management and authentication.
- To send notifications and offers.
- To improve app quality and develop features.
- To protect users and handle abusive or fraudulent behavior.
- **To verify the validity of recharge and payment requests and prevent fraud.**
- **To maintain a secure historical record of financial transactions.**

---

## 3. Third Parties

The app uses Firebase, Google AdMob, and other essential service providers to run analytics, notifications, and storage. Data is used with them only to support app functions, and we do not sell user data.

**Note**: We do not share financial transaction data with any third party unless required by law.

---

## 4. Permissions

- **Camera and Microphone**: To run voice and video calls within the app.
- **Photos and Video**: To upload and display content within the account.
- **Notifications**: To receive alerts and offers.
- **Internet**: To run the app and communicate with the server.

---

## 5. Security and Financial Data ⚠️

### 5.1 Financial Data Protection
- All recharge requests and transaction data are **encrypted with the highest security standards.**
- We use **end-to-end encryption** for sensitive data.
- **We do not save real card or wallet data.** We only save the transaction number, date, and amount for administrative verification.

### 5.2 Fraud Protection
- We apply limits on the number of recharge attempts to prevent abuse.
- We detect suspicious patterns.
- We may block suspicious accounts **temporarily or permanently.**

---

## 6. Children

- The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
- We do not accept any payment transactions from children's accounts.
- If you are a parent and believe a child's data has been collected without your consent, contact us immediately to delete it.
